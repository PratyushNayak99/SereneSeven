ASSAM

Kaziranga National Park

SERENE SEVEN

The coldest, windiest, most hostile continent. Only the toughest can survive here.

Heritage Site covering 430 square kilometers. Famous for hosting two-thirds of the world's one-horned rhinoceros population, it was established in 1905 and declared a national park in 1974. The park also houses tigers, elephants, and numerous diverse bird species.
